About MiniGamePlanet

MiniGamePlanet is a growing collection of free browser games you can play instantly — right here, right now, on whatever device you're reading this on. No app stores, no downloads, no accounts, no waiting. You open a page, you play a game. That's it.

We built MiniGamePlanet because we believe games should be accessible. Not locked behind paywalls, not buried in app store algorithms, not demanding your email before you can play. The web is the most universal platform in the world — every phone, tablet, and computer has a browser. So that's where we put our games.

What you'll find here

Our library spans a wide range of categories: arcade classics like Snake and Paddle Ball, brain-teasing puzzles like Sudoku and 2048, beloved card games like Solitaire and Blackjack, word games, strategy games, board games, casual games, action games, creative builder tools, math challenges, and trivia quizzes. Whether you have two minutes or two hours, there's something here for you.

Every game is built with vanilla web technology — HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No plugins, no frameworks, no dependencies that might break. The games are lightweight, fast, and work on any modern browser. Sound effects are generated procedurally using the Web Audio API (no audio files to download), and action games even support gamepad controllers if you have one plugged in.

Privacy first

We take your privacy seriously. All game data — scores, progress, preferences — lives entirely in your browser and never touches a server. We use analytics only with your explicit consent, and you can change your cookie preferences at any time using the "Cookie Settings" button in the footer. There are no hidden trackers, no data brokers, no dark patterns. Read our Privacy Policy for the full details.

Who's behind this?

MiniGamePlanet is operated by TPS Worldwide LLC, based in Phoenix, Arizona. We're a small team that believes the open web still has room for simple, fun experiences that respect the people using them.
