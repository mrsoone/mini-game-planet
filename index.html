What is MiniGamePlanet?

MiniGamePlanet is a growing collection of free browser games you can play instantly — no downloads, no accounts, no app store. From classic arcade games like Snake and Pong to brain-bending puzzles like Sudoku and 2048, strategy games like Chess and Checkers, card games like Solitaire and Blackjack, and everything in between. Every game runs right in your browser using standard web technology. Open a page, start playing. It's that simple.

📱

Works everywhere

Every game on MiniGamePlanet is designed to work on any device with a modern browser. Phone, tablet, laptop, desktop — pick your screen and play. Touch controls for mobile, keyboard and mouse for desktop, and even gamepad support for arcade and action games. The games adapt to your screen size automatically, so you always get the best experience whether you're on a 5-inch phone or a 27-inch monitor.

🔒

Your privacy, respected

MiniGamePlanet doesn't require accounts, doesn't store personal data, and doesn't send your information anywhere. All game logic runs entirely in your browser — your scores, your progress, your choices stay on your device. We use analytics cookies only with your explicit consent, and you can change your cookie preferences at any time from the footer. No tracking without permission, no dark patterns, no surprises.
