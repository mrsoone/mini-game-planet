How to Play

Space Rocks is a classic arcade space shooter. You control a triangular ship floating in a field of asteroids. Your goal is to destroy all asteroids on screen to advance to the next wave while avoiding collisions. The game rewards precision, timing, and spatial awareness.

  • Rotate: Use Arrow Left/Right or A/D to turn your ship. Rotation is instant—no momentum on turning.
  • Thrust: Press Arrow Up or W to accelerate forward. Your ship has momentum—release to coast. Use short bursts to maneuver.
  • Shoot: Press Space to fire. Bullets travel in a straight line and destroy asteroids on contact.
  • Wrap: Flying off one edge wraps you to the opposite side. Use this to escape tight situations.
  • Lives: You start with 3 lives. Hitting an asteroid costs a life. Survive as long as you can!

Large asteroids split into two medium asteroids when shot. Medium asteroids split into two small ones. Small asteroids are destroyed completely. Scoring: Large 20 points, Medium 50 points, Small 100 points. Destroy asteroids in quick succession to build a streak and maximize your score.

On mobile devices, use the on-screen buttons for rotation, thrust, and fire. The game adapts to touch screens for play anywhere.

History of Space Rock-Shooting Arcade Games

Classic space rock-shooting arcade games emerged in the late 1970s and became some of the most successful titles of all time. Featuring vector graphics displayed on black-and-white CRT monitors, they created a distinctive minimalist aesthetic that defined an era.

The genre's physics—ship inertia, wrap-around screens, and asteroid fragmentation—were innovative for the era. Players could drift through space, building momentum before firing, which added strategic depth. The increasing difficulty and hypnotic gameplay made these titles quarter-eating staples in arcades worldwide.

Space Rocks captures the spirit of these classic space shooters while adding modern conveniences like touch controls and session statistics. Play free in your browser and experience the thrill of the original arcade genre.

Strategy Tips

  • Keep moving: A stationary ship is an easy target. Use short thrust bursts to change direction.
  • Prioritize large asteroids: Breaking them early reduces screen clutter and creates more manageable fragments.
  • Use the wrap: When surrounded, thrust toward an edge to escape and reappear on the opposite side.
  • Lead your shots: Space rocks move; aim where they'll be, not where they are.
  • Build streaks: Destroy asteroids in quick succession for bonus points.

FAQ

How many lives do I get?

You start with 3 lives. Each collision with an asteroid costs one life. When all lives are gone, the game ends.

Do waves get harder?

Yes. Each wave adds more asteroids and increases their speed. Later waves require faster reflexes and better planning.

Can I play on mobile?

Yes. On-screen touch buttons appear on smaller screens for rotation, thrust, and fire controls.

What is the streak bonus?

Destroying asteroids in quick succession builds a streak. Higher streaks can earn bonus points and track your hot runs.
