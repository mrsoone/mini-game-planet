How to Play Whack-a-Mole

Whack-a-Mole is a timeless arcade classic that tests your reflexes and hand-eye coordination. Moles pop up from holes in a 3×3 grid—your job is to click or tap them before they disappear. Each successful hit earns you points, but watch out for bombs that will cost you! The game runs for 30 seconds, and your goal is to rack up as many points as possible while avoiding costly mistakes.

Scoring

  • 🐹 Regular mole: 1 point per hit
  • ⭐ Golden mole: 3 points per hit (3× bonus!)
  • 💣 Bomb: −5 points if you hit it—avoid these!

Golden moles are rare but rewarding. When you see that star, prioritize it—but only if you're confident you won't accidentally tap a bomb in the next hole. Bombs can quickly erase a good run, so a split-second pause to confirm what you're hitting can save your score.

Difficulty Levels

Choose Easy for a relaxed pace with fewer bombs and slower mole appearances. Normal offers balanced gameplay for most players. Hard mode increases mole speed and bomb frequency—ideal for seasoned players seeking a real challenge. As time passes in any mode, moles pop up more frequently, so the final seconds are always the most intense.

Tips & Strategy

Build combos by hitting moles in quick succession—your streak counter rewards consistency and helps you stay in the zone. The game runs for 30 seconds, so every second counts. Focus on golden moles when they appear, but never sacrifice accuracy for speed when a bomb might be lurking. Keep your cursor or finger near the center of the grid to minimize travel time. A Brief History

Whack-a-Mole originated in Japanese arcades in the 1970s and became a staple of carnivals and amusement parks worldwide. The physical game used mechanical moles that popped up from holes; players wielded mallets to bonk them before they retreated. The digital version you're playing captures that same quick-hit excitement in your browser. No downloads, no sign-ups—just pure arcade fun. Whether you're killing time, warming up your reflexes, or chasing a new high score, Whack-a-Mole delivers instant gratification. Enjoy!
