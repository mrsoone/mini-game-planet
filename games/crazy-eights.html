How to Play Crazy Eights

Crazy Eights is a classic shedding card game — the precursor to UNO. Players take turns playing a card that matches the suit or rank of the top discard card. Eights are wild and can be played on anything, letting you choose the next suit. First to empty their hand wins. If you can't play, draw from the deck until you can (or the deck runs out).

How to Play

Play a card from your hand that matches either the suit or rank of the top card on the discard pile. Eights are wild — play one to change the active suit. If you can't play, click "Draw Card" to draw. The AI opponent follows the same rules. First player with no cards wins.
