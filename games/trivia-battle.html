How to Play Trivia Battle

Trivia Battle is a competitive quiz game. All players see the same question simultaneously. The faster you answer correctly, the more points you earn. First correct answer gets full points; second gets half. 10 questions per round across a variety of categories.

Scoring

  • First correct: 100 points (speed bonus up to +50)
  • Second correct: 50 points
  • Wrong answer: 0 points
  • 15 seconds per question — answer fast!
