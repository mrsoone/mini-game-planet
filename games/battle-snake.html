🐍 Battle Snake

Eat, grow, outlast — multiplayer snake arena

🟢 You: 3 🔴 Enemy: 3 Speed: 1x
Arrow Keys or WASD to steer | Don't crash into walls or snakes!

How to Play Battle Snake

Battle Snake is classic Snake but multiplayer. Both snakes are on the same board, competing for food and survival. Eat food to grow longer. Crash into a wall, yourself, or the other snake and you're out. Last snake alive wins.

Rules

  • Steer with Arrow Keys or WASD
  • Eat the red food to grow longer
  • Speed increases as snakes get longer — the game gets harder over time
  • Crash into anything and you lose

Power-ups

  • ⚡ Speed Boost — temporary burst of speed
  • 👻 Ghost — pass through the other snake briefly
  • 🧲 Magnet — food is attracted toward you
