How to Play

Select your bet size (1 to 20 credits), pick a card theme (Fruit, Lucky, or Diamond), and click "Buy New Card." A 3×3 grid of symbols will be hidden beneath a silver scratch coating. Use your mouse or touch to scratch away the coating and reveal the symbols. The more you scratch, the more you reveal. Match three or more of the same symbol to win. If you don't want to scratch manually, scratch at least 70% of the card to unlock the "Reveal All" button and instantly see your results.

Canvas-Based Scratching

The scratch mechanic is built with HTML5 Canvas. A silver coating is drawn over the symbols, and as you drag your mouse or finger, the coating is "erased" to reveal what's underneath. The scratch radius and sensitivity are tuned for smooth, responsive feedback on both desktop and mobile devices. Touch support means you can scratch with your finger on phones and tablets.

Fair and Random

Every card uses the Web Crypto API's crypto.getRandomValues() to generate unpredictable symbol placement. This ensures that no two cards are alike and that the results cannot be predicted or manipulated. You get a genuine, fair gaming experience every time.
