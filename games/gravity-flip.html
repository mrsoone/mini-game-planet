Gravity Flip: The Ultimate Flip Runner

Gravity Flip is a fast-paced side-scrolling action game that puts a fresh spin on the endless runner genre. Instead of jumping over obstacles, you flip gravity itself—running along the ceiling when the floor is blocked, and back to the floor when the ceiling gets dangerous. One tap, click, or spacebar press is all it takes to switch worlds.

The game starts at a comfortable pace, but as your distance increases, so does the speed. Obstacles appear on both the floor and ceiling: spikes, blocks, and hazards that require split-second timing to avoid. Gaps in the platforms add another layer of danger—fall through a hole in the floor and you're done. The key is to anticipate what's ahead and flip at exactly the right moment.

Parallax scrolling backgrounds give the game depth and polish. Multiple layers move at different speeds as you run, creating a sense of motion and immersion. The visual feedback is clear: you always know whether you're on the floor or ceiling, and obstacles are easy to read even at high speeds.

Scoring is simple: your score equals the distance you've traveled. The farther you go, the higher your score—and the faster the game gets. Personal bests are saved in your browser, so you can always try to beat your record. Session stats track how many games you've played in one sitting, perfect for quick play sessions or marathon runs.

Whether you're on desktop or mobile, Gravity Flip is built to feel responsive. Touch controls work great on phones and tablets, while keyboard and mouse work seamlessly on computers. The game loop is optimized for smooth 60fps gameplay, and the canvas-based rendering keeps things lightweight and fast.

Ready to flip? Hit play, tap to flip gravity, and see how far you can run. Every run is different thanks to procedural obstacle placement, so no two games feel the same. Master the rhythm, learn the patterns, and push your score higher. Gravity Flip—where up and down are just suggestions.
