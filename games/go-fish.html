How to Play Go Fish

Go Fish is a classic card game for all ages. Each player starts with 7 cards. On your turn, ask the opponent for a specific rank (you must have at least one card of that rank). If they have any, they hand them all over. If not, they say "Go Fish!" and you draw from the pool. When you collect all four cards of a rank, that's a "set" — place it aside. The player with the most sets when all cards are matched wins.

How to Play

Click any card in your hand to ask the AI for that rank. If the AI has matching cards, they're transferred to you. If not, you draw from the fish pool. The AI then takes its turn automatically. Collect sets of four to score points.
