About Sky Bounce

Sky Bounce is a fast-paced tap-to-fly arcade game. You control a bird that must navigate through a series of pipes by tapping or clicking to flap and gain altitude. Gravity constantly pulls your bird downward, so timing is everything. Each pipe you pass adds one point to your score. Hit a pipe, the ground, or the ceiling and it's instant game over. The game rewards precision, reflexes, and persistence. With zero-friction restart, you can jump straight back into the action and chase your personal best.

How to Play

Your bird starts on the left side of the screen. Press Space, Up arrow, or tap/click anywhere to flap and gain upward momentum. Release and gravity takes over, pulling the bird down. Pipes scroll in from the right with a gap in the middle—fly through the gap to score a point. The bird rotates based on its velocity: nose up when rising, nose down when falling. This visual feedback helps you judge your trajectory. Avoid hitting the top or bottom pipes, the ground, or the ceiling. There are no lives—one collision and the game ends. The challenge is to see how many pipes you can pass before making a mistake.

Controls

Use any of these inputs to flap: Space bar, Up arrow key, mouse click anywhere on the game area, or tap on touch devices. The controls are designed for instant response—no holding, just tap to flap. On mobile, the entire canvas is a tap zone, so you can play one-handed. The game works on desktop and mobile with full touch support.

Scoring and Stats

You earn one point for each pipe you successfully pass. Your personal best is saved in the browser and persists between sessions. Session stats track how many games you've played in the current browser tab. Streak counts consecutive games where you beat your previous score—a streak of 3 means your last three games each improved on the one before. These stats add a layer of progression and give you goals beyond the raw score.

Tips for High Scores

Stay centered in the pipe gap—don't scrape the edges. Flap in short bursts rather than rapid tapping; over-flapping sends you into the ceiling. Let gravity do the work between pipes; conserve flaps for when you need to rise. Watch the bird's rotation to anticipate when you'll need to flap next. The pipes are randomly placed using secure random values, so each run is unique. Practice the rhythm: flap, glide, flap, glide. Zero-friction restart means you can immediately try again and build muscle memory.

Technical Notes

Sky Bounce runs entirely in your browser using HTML5 Canvas and requestAnimationFrame for smooth 60fps gameplay. Pipe positions are generated with crypto.getRandomValues() for fair, unpredictable obstacles. The ground scrolls for a polished visual effect. No downloads, no ads interrupting your run—just pure arcade action. Whether you're chasing a new high score or killing a few minutes, Sky Bounce delivers quick, addictive gameplay.
