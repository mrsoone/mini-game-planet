Capital Quiz

How well do you know the capitals of the world? Our free capital city quiz challenges you to match countries with their capital cities. Whether you're a geography buff preparing for a test, a traveler planning your next adventure, or simply curious about world capitals, this trivia game offers an engaging way to learn and test your knowledge.

Each round consists of 20 questions, with 15 seconds to answer each one. Correct answers earn points, and consecutive correct answers build a streak bonus—so stay focused! You can filter questions by region: play globally or focus on Europe, Asia, the Americas, Africa, or Oceania. Over 100 country-capital pairs are included, from Tokyo and Paris to Brasília and Canberra.

Choose your preferred play mode: Multiple Choice shows four options for each country, while Type-In mode lets you type the capital name yourself for a tougher challenge. Your personal best score is saved locally, so you can track your progress and compete against yourself over time. The game uses cryptographically secure random number generation via crypto.getRandomValues() to ensure fair, unpredictable question selection.

Learning world capitals builds geographic literacy and cultural awareness. Many capitals are ancient cities with rich histories—Rome, Athens, and Cairo have been centers of civilization for millennia. Others, like Washington D.C., Brasília, and Canberra, were purpose-built as capital cities. Our quiz covers capitals from every inhabited continent, including European classics like London and Berlin, Asian megacities such as Tokyo and Beijing, African capitals from Cairo to Pretoria, and island nations across Oceania.

Scoring rewards both accuracy and speed. Each correct answer earns base points plus a bonus that decreases as the timer runs down. String together correct answers to build a streak—every consecutive correct answer increases your multiplier. A five-question streak can nearly double your points per question. Miss one or run out of time and the streak resets. In Type-In mode, answers are matched flexibly: minor spelling variations and common alternate names (e.g., "Colombo" for Sri Lanka) are accepted.

Whether you're preparing for a geography exam, brushing up before a trip, or just enjoy a quick mental challenge, our capital quiz delivers. No sign-up required—just select your region, choose your mode, hit Start, and begin. Sound effects can be toggled on or off for a personalized experience. Your session statistics track total correct answers and best streak across rounds. Ready to prove your capital expertise? Start a round and see how many you can identify!
