What Is a Clicker Game?

A clicker game, also known as an idle game or incremental game, is a casual genre where players earn points or currency by clicking a button. The core loop is simple: click to earn, spend earnings on upgrades, and watch your income grow. What makes clickers addictive is the sense of constant progress—even when you're not actively playing, auto-clickers and passive income keep the numbers climbing.

Our Clicker Game follows this classic formula. You start with a single point per click. As you accumulate points, you can purchase three types of upgrades: Click Power doubles your per-click value, Auto Clicker adds passive income per second, and the Multiplier boosts all income by 25%. Each upgrade becomes more expensive as you buy more, creating a satisfying challenge of balancing immediate gains with long-term investments.

The prestige system adds another layer of strategy. When you reach 10,000 points, you can reset your progress in exchange for a permanent 10% bonus to all income. This bonus stacks with each prestige, so dedicated players can eventually reach astronomical numbers. Prestige rewards patience and planning—knowing when to reset versus when to push for one more upgrade is part of the fun.

Idle games have become hugely popular on mobile and web. Titles like Cookie Clicker, Adventure Capitalist, and Idle Miner have millions of players. They're perfect for short sessions—check in, click a few times, buy an upgrade, and come back later. The low barrier to entry and satisfying feedback loops make them ideal for casual gamers and anyone who enjoys watching numbers go up.

Strategy matters in clicker games. Early on, focus on Click Power to maximize each tap. Once you have a steady income, Auto Clickers become valuable—they work while you're away. The Multiplier is a force multiplier for everything, so buying it when you can afford it accelerates your growth. Before prestiging, consider whether one more upgrade will pay off faster than resetting.

Our version runs entirely in your browser with no downloads required. Your progress is saved automatically to your device, so you can pick up right where you left off. Whether you're killing time during a commute or optimizing your prestige strategy, the Clicker Game offers a relaxing yet engaging experience. Give it a try and see how high you can climb!
