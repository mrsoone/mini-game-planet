Pixel Art Painter: Create Stunning Pixel Art Online

Pixel art has made a triumphant comeback in modern gaming and digital design. From indie games like Stardew Valley and Celeste to nostalgic retro aesthetics, pixel art continues to captivate creators and audiences alike. Our Pixel Art Painter gives you a powerful, free online tool to create pixel art without installing any software.

Whether you're designing game sprites, creating avatars, or exploring a new creative hobby, this pixel art editor provides everything you need. Choose from three grid sizes—8×8 for tiny icons and emojis, 16×16 for classic retro sprites, or 32×32 for more detailed artwork. Each size offers the perfect canvas for different projects.

Our intuitive toolset includes a draw tool for placing pixels one at a time, a flood fill tool for quickly coloring large areas, an erase tool for corrections, and an eyedropper to sample colors from your canvas. The built-in color palette features 16+ carefully selected colors, plus a custom color picker so you can match any shade you imagine.

Made a mistake? The undo feature stores your last 20 actions, so you can experiment freely without fear. Toggle grid lines on or off to see your artwork with or without guides. When you're done, download your creation as a PNG file—perfect for sharing on social media, using in game development, or printing.

Pixel art teaches valuable design principles: working within constraints breeds creativity, and every pixel matters. Start small with an 8×8 grid to learn the basics, then scale up as your skills grow. The Pixel Art Painter runs entirely in your browser—no account required, no limits on how many artworks you create.

Join thousands of creators who use Mini Game Planet's tools to bring their pixel art visions to life. Whether you're a seasoned pixel artist or just starting out, our editor makes the process simple and enjoyable.
