Why Use an Online Dice Roller?

Online dice rollers offer convenience, fairness, and accessibility. You can roll anywhere, anytime, without hunting for lost dice under the couch. Our dice use cryptographically secure randomness via crypto.getRandomValues(), ensuring each roll is truly random—no predictable patterns or bias. That matters when the outcome of a game hangs on a single roll.

Tabletop gamers, educators, and casual players alike benefit from a reliable virtual dice. No more accusations of loaded dice or uneven surfaces. Every roll is generated using the same cryptographic standards that secure web transactions, so you can trust the results.

Statistics and Roll History

Curious whether the dice are fair? Our frequency distribution panel shows how often each face (1–6) has appeared across your session. How It Works

Select your game mode, choose the number of dice (or dice type for D&D), and click Roll. Each die animates with a tumbling effect before landing on its final value. Individual results and the total are displayed immediately. The interface is clean, fast, and works on desktop and mobile.

Six-sided dice display traditional pips (dots) in a 3D cube that tumbles before settling. Polyhedral dice show their numeric result in a clean, readable format. Sound effects are optional—toggle them on or off to suit your environment.
