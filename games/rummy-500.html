How to Play Rummy 500

Rummy 500 is a classic card game where the goal is to score points by forming melds — sets of matching cards and runs of consecutive cards in the same suit. Unlike basic Rummy, you score the point value of the cards you meld, and lose points for cards left in your hand when someone goes out. First to 500 wins.

Gameplay

With 2 players, deal 13 cards each. With 3-4 players, deal 7 cards each. On your turn: draw one card (from the stock or discard pile), optionally meld sets or runs (3+ cards), and discard one card. You can also lay off — add cards to any existing meld on the table.

Scoring

  • Aces: 15 points each
  • Face cards (J, Q, K): 10 points each
  • Number cards: face value
  • You score positive points for cards in melds you played
  • Cards remaining in your hand score negative

When a player empties their hand or the stock runs out, the round ends. Tally scores. First to 500 wins.

Strategy

  • Meld early to lock in points, but hold key cards if you're building a big hand.
  • Lay off aggressively. Adding to other players' melds scores you points.
  • Watch discards. Drawing from the discard pile reveals your strategy — only do it for valuable melds.
