Geography Quiz

How well do you know where countries are located on the world map? Our geography quiz challenges you to pinpoint countries by clicking on a simplified world map. Each round shows a country name—your job is to click where you think that country is located. The closer your guess, the more points you earn. It's a fun way to improve your mental map of the world while testing your geographic knowledge.

The game uses a simplified SVG world map with continent outlines and clickable areas. When you click, a line appears showing the distance between your guess and the correct location. A green marker indicates the right spot, so you learn as you play. Scoring rewards precision: a perfect or near-perfect click can earn up to 100 points, while guesses farther away earn fewer points. Twenty rounds per game keep sessions engaging without being overwhelming.

Your running score is displayed throughout the round, and your personal best is saved locally in your browser. Challenge yourself to beat your high score over time. The game uses cryptographically secure random number generation via crypto.getRandomValues() to ensure fair, unpredictable country selection. No two games are alike.

Geography literacy matters. Understanding where countries are located helps with travel planning, news comprehension, and cultural awareness. Many people can name capitals but struggle to place countries on a map. This quiz bridges that gap by making location practice interactive and rewarding. From Brazil and Japan to Egypt and Australia, you'll encounter countries from every inhabited continent.

The simplified map uses an equirectangular projection—a common way to flatten the globe onto a 2D surface. Latitude and longitude lines are implied by the layout: the top of the map is the Arctic, the bottom is Antarctica, and the horizontal span covers 360 degrees of longitude. Major landmasses are outlined so you can orient yourself quickly. Whether you're a geography buff or a casual learner, the quiz adapts to your pace.

Sound effects can be toggled on or off for a personalized experience. No sign-up required—just hit Start and begin clicking. Perfect for a quick brain break, classroom use, or brushing up before a trip. Ready to prove your geographic expertise? Locate 20 countries and see how high you can score!
