Trivia Quiz

Test your knowledge with our free trivia quiz! Whether you're a science buff, history enthusiast, or pop culture expert, our game offers something for everyone. Each round features 10 randomly selected questions from a pool of 50+ across six categories: Science, History, Geography, Entertainment, Sports, and Technology.

Every question comes with a 15-second countdown. Answer quickly to earn speed bonuses on top of your base score. Build consecutive correct answers to activate streak multipliers and watch your score soar. If you select the wrong answer, the correct one is highlighted in green so you can learn as you play.

Your personal best score is saved in your browser, so you can challenge yourself to beat your own record. Session statistics track your performance across rounds, including total correct answers, average response time, and longest streak. We use secure randomness via crypto.getRandomValues to ensure fair question selection.

Science questions cover chemistry, physics, biology, and astronomy. History spans ancient civilizations, world wars, and notable figures. Geography tests your knowledge of capitals, landmarks, and natural wonders. Entertainment includes film, music, TV, and literature. Sports covers rules, records, and legendary athletes. Technology explores computing, the internet, and innovation.

Scoring rewards both accuracy and speed. Each correct answer earns base points plus a bonus that decreases as the timer runs down. String together correct answers to build a streak—every consecutive correct answer increases your multiplier. A five-question streak can nearly double your points per question. Miss one and the streak resets, so stay focused!

All questions are multiple choice with four options. Wrong answers reveal the correct one in green, so you learn something new even when you miss. Rounds are short enough for a coffee break but challenging enough to keep you coming back. Compete against your own high score or share your results with friends.

The trivia quiz is designed for casual play—no account required, no ads blocking the experience. Just load the page, hit Start, and see how much you know. Perfect for a quick brain break, a friendly competition with friends, or brushing up on facts across multiple subjects. Good luck, and have fun!
