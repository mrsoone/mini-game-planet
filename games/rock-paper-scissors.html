Rock Paper Scissors: The Timeless Hand Game You Can Play Online

Rock Paper Scissors is one of the world's most beloved hand games. Whether you're settling a dispute, making a quick decision, or simply having fun, this classic game has been a staple of playgrounds, classrooms, and casual gatherings for generations. Now you can enjoy it anytime, anywhere, right in your browser—no opponent needed.

How to Play Rock Paper Scissors

The rules are beautifully simple. Two players simultaneously form one of three shapes with their hand: Rock (a fist), Paper (an open palm), or Scissors (a peace sign). Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beat Paper, and Paper beats Rock. If both players choose the same shape, it's a draw. The game requires no equipment, no setup, and no prior experience—just quick thinking and a bit of luck.

The Lizard-Spock Expansion

Popularized by The Big Bang Theory, the Lizard-Spock variant adds two more choices: Lizard and Spock. This creates a more complex web of outcomes. Scissors cut Paper and decapitate Lizard. Paper covers Rock and disproves Spock. Rock crushes Lizard and Scissors. Lizard poisons Spock and eats Paper. Spock smashes Scissors and vaporizes Rock. With five options instead of three, the game becomes more strategic and less predictable.

Best of 5 Mode

For a more competitive experience, try our Best of 5 mode. The first player to win three rounds takes the match. This format reduces the impact of a single lucky guess and rewards consistency.
