Card Dodger: The Ultimate Reflex Challenge

Card Dodger is a thrilling browser-based action game that tests your reflexes and hand-eye coordination. As cards and shapes rain down from the top of the screen, your job is simple: survive. Move your character left and right along the bottom of the play area, dodging every obstacle that comes your way. The longer you last, the higher your score—and the faster the objects fall.

What makes Card Dodger stand out is its blend of classic arcade mechanics with modern power-up systems. While dodging is your primary goal, you'll want to actively seek out power-ups that appear among the falling objects. The shield grants temporary invincibility, letting you pass through obstacles unscathed. Slow-mo reduces the speed of falling objects, giving you precious extra time to react. The magnet pulls nearby coins toward you, boosting your bonus score without risky maneuvers.

Coins scattered throughout the game add an extra layer of strategy. Do you risk moving toward a coin and potentially into the path of a card? Or play it safe and focus purely on survival? The choice is yours. Your final score combines time survived with coins collected, so balancing risk and reward is key to reaching the top of the leaderboard.

Card Dodger supports multiple control schemes to suit your preference. Use the arrow keys or A and D for keyboard control, or simply click and drag on the game area for mouse and touch support. This makes the game accessible on desktop and mobile devices alike. Whether you're on a lunch break or commuting, a quick game of Card Dodger is always within reach.

The game features session statistics including your personal best score, current streak of games played, and total games this session. Your best score is saved locally so you can always strive to beat your record. With procedurally generated obstacles and increasing difficulty, no two games are exactly alike. Card Dodger is free to play, requires no installation, and runs entirely in your browser. Give it a try and see how long you can last!
