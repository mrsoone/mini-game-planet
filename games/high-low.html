How to Play High Low

High Low is the simplest card game imaginable — and one of the most addictive. A card is shown face-up. Guess whether the next card will be higher or lower. Correct guesses build your streak. One wrong guess and it's over. How long can you keep your streak alive?

How to Play

Click "Higher" if you think the next card's rank will be higher, or "Lower" if you think it will be lower. Cards rank from 2 (lowest) to Ace (highest). If the next card is equal, it counts as a loss. The game uses a single shuffled deck — once a card is played, it won't appear again. Use this knowledge: if the current card is a King, the next one is almost certainly lower.

Strategy

  • Low cards → guess Higher. If the current card is a 3, there are many more cards above it.
  • High cards → guess Lower. A Queen or King has most of the deck below it.
  • Middle cards are the gamble. A 7 or 8 is nearly 50/50. Go with your gut.
  • Track what's been played. With a single deck, you can mentally count which ranks remain.
