About Space Invaders

Space Invaders is one of the most iconic arcade games ever created. Originally released by Taito in 1978, it helped define the shoot-'em-up genre and became a cultural phenomenon. The game's distinctive pixelated aliens and relentless march toward Earth captured the imagination of players worldwide. In this browser version, you'll experience the same addictive gameplay that captivated millions of players decades ago—no quarters required.

How to Play

Your mission is simple: destroy all invading aliens before they reach Earth. You control a laser cannon at the bottom of the screen. Move left and right to dodge enemy fire while shooting upward to eliminate the alien formations. The aliens march in rows, moving side to side and stepping down each time they hit the screen edge. As you clear waves, the aliens move faster and become more aggressive. Use arrow keys or A/D to move, and press Space or tap the screen to shoot.

Game Features

Protective shields provide temporary cover but erode when hit by either your shots or alien bullets. Use them wisely—they won't last forever. You have three lives; lose them all and it's game over. Aliens will occasionally shoot back, so stay alert and keep moving. Your score increases with each alien destroyed, with bonus points for higher rows. Build combos for streak bonuses and try to beat your personal best! Session stats track your games played and cumulative score.

Tips for High Scores

Prioritize the bottom rows first—they're worth more points and removing them reduces incoming fire from above. Time your shots to hit multiple aliens as they align horizontally. Don't waste shots on shields when you can shoot around them. As waves progress, the pace quickens; stay calm and focus on accuracy over speed. Zero-friction restart lets you jump straight back into the action after game over.

Touch and Keyboard Controls

On desktop, use arrow keys or A/D to move and Space to shoot. On mobile, the screen is split into left and right halves—touch and hold the left side to move left, the right side to move right. Tap anywhere for a quick shot. The responsive layout works on phones and tablets.

Classic Arcade Legacy

Space Invaders pioneered many concepts still used in games today: progressive difficulty, high-score competition, and simple yet compelling mechanics. It sparked the golden age of arcade gaming and influenced countless titles that followed. Play free in your browser—no downloads, no ads interrupting your run.
