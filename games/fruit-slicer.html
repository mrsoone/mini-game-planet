How to Play Fruit Slicer

Fruit Slicer is an addictive arcade game inspired by the classic fruit-slicing genre. Colorful fruits launch upward from the bottom of the screen, and your job is to slice them before they fall off the top. Use your mouse or finger to swipe across fruits—each successful slice adds to your score. But beware: bombs occasionally appear among the fruit. Slice a bomb and it's game over!

Game Mechanics

Fruits spawn at the bottom and arc upward. You have three lives—lose one each time a fruit escapes off the top of the screen unsliced. Slice multiple fruits in quick succession to build combos and earn bonus points. The more fruits you slice in one swipe, the higher your combo multiplier. Your personal best score is saved for the session, and you can track your streak of consecutive games.

Fruits and Bombs

The game features five fruit types: apple, orange, watermelon, banana, and pear. Each appears as a colored circle with an emoji. Bombs look different—slice them and the game ends immediately. Stay alert and only slice what you're sure is fruit!

Tips for High Scores

Build combos by slicing multiple fruits in a single swipe. Plan your cuts to hit several fruits at once. Watch the trajectory of fruits and time your swipes. Avoid rushing—accuracy matters more than speed when bombs are on screen. Use the visible blade trail to aim your slices precisely.

Controls

On desktop, click and drag across the screen to slice. On mobile, use touch and swipe. A visible trail shows your blade path. The game runs smoothly on both desktop and mobile browsers.
