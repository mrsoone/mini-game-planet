How to Play Tank Battle

Tank Battle is a top-down arena shooter. Control your tank, navigate around walls, and blast your opponent. Destructible brick walls can be shot through; steel walls are permanent. Power-ups spawn on the field — grab them for rapid fire, shields, and speed boosts.

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys — Move and aim your tank
  • Space — Fire a bullet in the direction you're facing
  • On mobile: use the on-screen joystick and fire button

Power-ups

  • ⚡ Rapid Fire — Shoot faster for 8 seconds
  • 🛡️ Shield — Absorb one hit
  • 🏎️ Speed Boost — Move 50% faster for 8 seconds

Each match lasts 2 minutes. The tank with the most kills wins. If tied, it's sudden death!
