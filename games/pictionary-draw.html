How to Play Quick Draw

Quick Draw is a Pictionary-style drawing and guessing game. One player draws a secret word on the canvas while others type guesses. The faster you guess correctly, the more points you earn. After 60 seconds, the round ends and the answer is revealed. 5 rounds per game with alternating drawers.

Scoring

  • Correct guess: Points based on speed — 100 pts at 60s down to 10 pts at the last second
  • Drawing bonus: Drawer gets 50 pts when someone guesses correctly
  • Hints appear at 40s and 20s remaining, revealing some letters
