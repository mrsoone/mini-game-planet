Flag Quiz: Test Your World Flag Knowledge

Identify country flags from around the globe

How well do you know the flags of the world? Our free flag quiz challenges you to identify country flags from six continents. Whether you're a geography enthusiast or simply curious about world cultures, this trivia game offers an engaging way to learn and test your knowledge.

Each round consists of 20 questions, with 10 seconds to answer each one. Correct answers earn points, and consecutive correct answers build a streak bonus—so stay focused! You can filter questions by region: play globally or focus on Europe, Asia, the Americas, Africa, or Oceania. Over 50 major country flags are included, from the iconic stars and stripes of the United States to the rising sun of Japan, the maple leaf of Canada, and the green and gold of Australia.

Flags are rendered using CSS and SVG-style divs, approximating real flag designs with colored stripes, circles, and geometric patterns. This approach keeps the game lightweight and fast-loading while still providing recognizable representations. Your personal best score is saved locally, so you can track your progress and compete against yourself over time.

The game uses cryptographically secure random number generation via crypto.getRandomValues() to ensure fair, unpredictable question selection. Sound effects can be toggled on or off for a personalized experience.

Learning to recognize national flags is more than a party trick—it builds cultural awareness and geographic literacy. Many flags tell stories: the tricolor of France reflects revolutionary ideals, Japan's hinomaru evokes the rising sun, and the stars and stripes of the USA represent states and original colonies. Our quiz covers flags from every inhabited continent, including European classics like Germany and Italy, Asian powerhouses such as China and India, African nations from Egypt to South Africa, and island nations across Oceania.

Whether you're preparing for a geography test, brushing up before a trip, or just enjoy a quick mental challenge, our flag quiz delivers. No sign-up required—just click Start and begin. Ready to prove your flag expertise? Start a round and see how many you can identify!
