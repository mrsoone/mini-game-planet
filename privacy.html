1. Introduction

Welcome to MiniGamePlanet ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by TPS Worldwide LLC, based in Phoenix, Arizona. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit and use our website at minigameplanet.com (the "Site"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Site.

2. Information We Collect

Game Usage Data (100% Client-Side)

All game logic, scores, preferences, and progress data are processed and stored entirely within your browser. This data never leaves your device. We do not have access to your game scores, settings, or any other in-game data. When you close your browser tab or clear your browser data, this information is gone.

Analytics Data (With Your Consent)

If you consent to analytics cookies, we use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymized usage data such as pages visited, time on site, general geographic region, device type, and browser type. This data helps us understand which games are popular and how to improve the Site. Analytics data is never collected without your explicit consent.

Advertising Data (With Your Consent)

If you consent to advertising cookies, Google AdSense may collect data to serve relevant advertisements. No advertising scripts are loaded unless you explicitly grant advertising consent through our cookie consent banner.

3. Cookies

We use a minimal number of cookies. Our Cookie Policy provides a complete table of all cookies used on this Site, including their provider, purpose, duration, and whether they require your consent. The only cookie that does not require consent is the functional cookie that stores your cookie preference itself.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Analytics data is used to understand Site traffic, identify popular games, improve user experience, and monitor Site performance.
  • Advertising data is used by Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements that support the free operation of this Site.
  • Cookie preference data is used to remember your consent choices so we do not ask you on every visit.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade any personal information to third parties.
  • We do not use data for profiling or automated decision-making beyond ad personalization (with consent).

6. Your Rights Under GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right of Access — You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification — You can request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure — You can request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Data Portability — You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object — You can object to data processing, including opting out of analytics and advertising cookies at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the footer.
  • Right to Restrict Processing — You can request that we limit how we use your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address provided in Section 10 below.

7. Your Rights Under CCPA (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Know — You can request disclosure of what personal information we collect, use, and share.
  • Right to Delete — You can request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out — You can opt out of the "sale" of personal information. Note: we do not sell personal information. However, certain advertising cookies may constitute a "sale" under CCPA's broad definition. You can opt out via our cookie consent banner.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

8. Children's Privacy

MiniGamePlanet is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the Site does not require accounts or collect personal data by default, there is minimal risk. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information to us through any means, please contact us and we will take steps to delete that information.

9. Data Retention

Analytics data collected by Google Analytics 4 is retained for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted. Your cookie consent preference is stored in your browser's localStorage and persists until you clear your browser data or change your preference via the "Cookie Settings" button in the footer. We do not maintain any server-side database of user data.
